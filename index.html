Akwa Ibom State

Akwa Ibom is one of the states in Nigeria. It is bordered by the Atlantic Ocean to the south, Rivers State to the west, Abia State to the north-west, and Cross River State to the east. It is known as the land of Promise and one of the oil producing states in the country. It has the best stadium in the country and the Nigeria Football Federation considers it as the major stadium to be used by the Super Eagles of Nigeria during football matches.
